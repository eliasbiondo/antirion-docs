# Platform operator

The platform-operator audience role resolves to Antirion staff accounts in saas mode and to principals holding the platform admin role on a self_hosted install. Platform-operator surfaces are EPIC-013 features.

See `_policies/notifications_model.md` and `_policies/deployment_modes.md`.
